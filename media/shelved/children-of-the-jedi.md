I'm currently (off and on) going through all the "Expanded Universe" Star Wars media, starting with the release of Timothy Zahn's 1991 masterpiece *Heir to the Empire*. *Children of the Jedi* was no masterpiece. While it did have it's moments, most of them taking place in the final hundred pages, the majority of this book was simultaneously slow and hard to follow. There were some interesting ideas sparingly scattered throughout, some having to do with the force, which I'm always a fan of reading about. And this book gets weird, man—and not just in bad ways. Still, **I do not recommend it.**
